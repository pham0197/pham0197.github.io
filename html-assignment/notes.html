HTML Page Layout

HTML Tags
Name Descriptions
Header Used as the child of Body tag it acts as the master header of the page. When added inside the Aticle tag it acts as the header for the article.
Footer Used as the child of Body tag it acts as the footer of the web page. When added inside the Article tag it acts as the footer for the article.
Nav The navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website
Main Primary content of the page
Article Is the tag used for containing independent piece of content. It could be shared as stand alone piece of content and will still make sense.
Aside Secondary content on the page, this information is not part of the main topic of the page and is not required.
Section Group of related content on the page. A section must have its own heading.
